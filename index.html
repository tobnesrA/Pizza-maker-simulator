📖 Project Description

This game is a step-by-step pizza cooking simulator. The player must choose basic ingredients, add them in the correct sequence, prepare the dough and filling, and then serve the finished dish to customers. At the beginning, only basic products are available, but as you progress through the game, new ingredients and recipes unlock.

The player must carefully follow the steps: choosing ingredients, placing them correctly, and the cooking process. If the order is violated or something is done incorrectly, the pizza may turn out poor quality.

The game is designed for gradual complexity. As you progress, the number of orders increases, more complex recipes are added, and time management elements appear. This makes the gameplay dynamic and interesting.

Why Play Pizza Cooking Simulator?

The game can be viewed as practical training: the player learns to plan actions, understand process sequencing, and manage limited resources.
