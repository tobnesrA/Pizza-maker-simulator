📖 Project Description

This game is a step-by-step pizza cooking simulator. The player must choose basic ingredients, add them in the correct sequence, prepare the dough and filling, and then serve the finished dish to customers. At the beginning, only basic products are available, but as you progress through the game, new ingredients and recipes unlock, allowing you to cook different types of pizza.

The player must carefully follow the steps: choosing ingredients, placing them correctly, the cooking process, and serving orders. If the order is violated or something is done incorrectly, the pizza may turn out poor quality or fail inspection altogether.

The game is designed for gradual complexity. As you progress, the number of orders increases, more complex recipes are added, and time management elements appear. This makes the gameplay dynamic and interesting.

The interface is oriented toward easy learning: the first stages are easy and clear, and subsequent steps require more attention and tactics. Thanks to this approach, the player can fearlessly try different combinations and learn from their results.

Why Play Pizza Cooking Simulator?

The game can be viewed as practical training in the basics of cooking: even if real culinary skills don't develop directly, the player learns to plan actions, understand the sequence of processes, and consider limited resources.

Whether you're a casual gamer looking for a relaxing experience or someone interested in strategy games, Pizza Cooking Simulator offers engaging gameplay that challenges your planning and decision-making skills.
